## Prevention Measures for Phishing Emails

- Do not click on links from unknown or suspicious emails.
- Always check the sender’s email address carefully before responding.
- Never share passwords, OTPs, or personal information through email.
- Avoid downloading attachments from unfamiliar sources.
- Verify messages by visiting the official website instead of using email links.
- Look for spelling mistakes or unusual language in emails.
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) for important accounts.
- Keep your device and antivirus software updated.
- Do not respond to emails that create unnecessary urgency or fear.
- Report phishing emails and mark them as spam immediately.
